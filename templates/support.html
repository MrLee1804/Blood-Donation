Help & Support

To register as a donor, click on the "Register" link in the navigation menu or go to the registration page directly. Fill out the required information including your personal details, blood group, and contact information. Once submitted, you'll be registered in our system.

To donate blood, you must be at least 17 years old, weigh at least 110 pounds, and be in good general health. Some medical conditions or recent travel may temporarily prevent you from donating. Please consult with our staff if you have specific questions about eligibility.

You can donate whole blood every 56 days (8 weeks). Platelet donations can be made more frequently, up to 24 times per year. Our system will track your donations and let you know when you're eligible to donate again.

Our website maintains an updated calendar of blood donation events. You can view upcoming events on the dashboard page after logging in. Events are also announced through our email newsletter and social media channels.
